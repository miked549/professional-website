Understanding Laser Therapy

Laser lipolysis is a noninvasive method for reducing fat. Laser light beams penetrate the adipose cells and bore minute holes in the membrane while simultaneously emulsifying the lipids.

Over the next few weeks, the lipids drain through the pores and are processed by the body’s lymphatic system. The adipose cells are not killed during the process, so if the patient begins to acquire new fat, it can be evenly distributed throughout the body.

This type of laser therapy offers a precise and controlled approach to fat reduction. This ensures optimal results with no side effects, making these treatments highly desirable for clients, providing an advanced, non-invasive option for anyone who is looking to effectively reduce fat in targeted areas without the need for surgical interventions.
